About Us

Mazao Safi which means good harvest to a farmers and safe produce to a consumers is our division for private extension services for small and medium scale farmers. We established Mazao Safi to bridge the gap in knowledge and among farmers in order to improve their Productivity, Product Quality and Profitability. Our extension services are structured around our team of technical assistants who hold a basic diploma in agriculture and are equipped with tools to offer farmers crop advice around soil fertility management, crop nutrition, pest and disease management, post-harvest and management of land resources including water and the local ecology. Each technical assistant works with 120-150 farmers who he visits individually at least once in 3 weeks and collectively offers trainings monthly through their different aggregations.
