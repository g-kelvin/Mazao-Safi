Welcome To Tradecare Lishe Safi

Lishe Safi is a consumer facing label that communicates quality to consumers and provides assurance that all food marketed under the label is produced in line with good agricultural standards through localGAP standard verification and processed in HACCP certified facilities.

Lishe Safi Deliverables

Our Solution

Lishe Safi is a consumer facing platform that links back to Mazao Safi to harness value for consumers while utilizing the Mazao Safi extension platform as a source of raw materials.

Curated Product Basket and Sourcing Strategy

Lishe Safi has already undertaken a consumer facing study and identified 36 products.

Full Traceability using Farm Data Pod System

Lishe Safi ensures that products sourced from farmers can be traced from farm to plate.

LocalGAP verification and product testing

Lishe Safi uses localGAP foundation standard as the basis for all food safety claims made on all food sold in the platform.

Hygienic Logistics Infrastructure

Lishe Safi will deploy fully refrigerated transportation infrastructure, HACCP certified processing facilities and hygienic packaging.
