How We Operate

Farmer Extesion Services

Mazao Safi Farmer Extension services are result driven and market oriented. Farmers are supported to sustainably improve their Productivity, Quality and Profitability while producing food safely.

Farmer Buying Centers

Mazao Safi farmers are aggregated into clusters with each cluster having between 50 and 200 farmers. The clusters are supported to set up buying centers for ease of aggregation and linkages to formal markets.

Controlled Logistics

Mazao Safi logistics organisation services are meant to ensure that product quality is maintained, marketable volumes are achieved at reasonable cost. Mazao Safi has invested in refrigerated transportation and has an open solicitation for logistics providers.

Hygienic Processing

The Mazao Safi Packhouse is located in Lower Kabette area of Kikuyu Subcounty Kiambu County. The packhouse setting allows for hygienic processing, collection of samples for pesticide residue and microbial analysis, value addition of products in line with customer requirements.

Licensed Grocery Stores

Mazao Safi is actively recruiting grocery stores interested to market food that is fully traceable, certified and tested to consumers. We will be listing grocers who carry food with our label across Nairobi City.

Mazao Safi

Mazao Safi is a consumer facing market that communicates the intrinsic values of full certification, product testing and Traceability. Consumers are encouraged to contact us for testing results, certification and traceability.
